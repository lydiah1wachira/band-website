EVANESCENCE

About

Evanescence is an American gothic rock band founded by singer and pianist Amy Lee and guitarist Ben Moody.

The band released their first album Fallen in 2003, which sold more than 17 million copies worldwide due to hit songs such as "Bring me to life" and "My Immortal" and won the band two Grammy Awards.

A year later, they release their first live album World Wide which sold more than two million copies

The band took a hiatus in 2009 and they returned in 2015 at a concert in Japan.
